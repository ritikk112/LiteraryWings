about us

Creating Successful Adults, One Child at a Time

Started by a group of determined, strongly minded, socially active and educated youths with the motive to provide quality education to the underprivileged children. Our motto “Ignite curious minds” clearly suggests our aim is to give the children an opportunity and a platform to utilise their full potential to learn and grow.

We have appointed volunteers in Delhi NCR who work to achieve the target of teaching 700-800 children daily. Apart from teaching, we, conduct activities and events on special occasions, distribute clothes and ration at regular intervals and provide financial support to the children. For more engagement of children, we also hold regular workshops and organise Sports fests.

Our Initiatives


Mission


To identify and educate children and make them self-dependent so that they can become dignified members of the society & have a common platform with others in terms of social equality and justice. To make children discover their hidden potentials so that they can be the change in society.

Motive


Our motto is “Educate Empower Enlighten”>. The members of the organization working together, providing the best fit education to these children. The seed of education will enable these children not just to grow but also bloom into responsible citizens of our country. This will empower them to stand on their own pillars in the society, to achieve success in life and rise as an enlightening star. The sincere motive of the organization is to bring overall development of the children.

Acheivement


Children who are admitted to school are performing extremely well in their academics. Till now we have admitted more than 40 children to schools. Our children are competing at National level. Few are winning State level sports championship in Boxing, Kho-Kho etc. whereas others are competing in prestigious academic institutions like JNV. Three of our kids cleared entrance in 2017 as well. Parallely, we are taking many initiatives for upbringing of our kids, you can find more on our project page.
